Profile

I am a developer, an Automation and Control (Mechatronics) Engineer and an entusiast of computer science.
During my research in graduation I worked with Rockwell PLC, designing and implementing control algorithms in Structured Text, Ladder and Functional Blocks.
I have been envolved with programming since 2011, when I started to work with low-level programming languages, remarkably C and Assembly. Since then I have studied many other languages and concepts, like Python, JS, POO and Real-Time Computing. In 2014 I joined the humanoid robotic soccer team UnBeatables, representing my university at the international robotic competition Robocup.
Since the beginning of last year I have been a member of the Moringa Digital development team, one of the largest web advertising and web systems companies in Brasilia. Nowadays, I am working with mobile hybrid development, using Ionic 2, Cordova and IntelXDK together with the back-end common tasks, using MySQL and ASP.
